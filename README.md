# Japanese Cultural Behavior Workshop @University of Iowa
As an Instructional Design Project, I conducted a workshop on Japanese cultural behavior, drawing on research-based frameworks. 

(1) Analysis: I analyzed instructional needs in learning Japanese as a foreign language, revealing that learners of the Japanese language often lack implicit cultural knowledge and do not notice a lack of knowledge. 

(2) Design & Development: I designed and developed an instructional sequence based on the ADDIE Model and Backward Design.

(3) Implementation: I implemented the Cultural Behavior Workshop on December 17th in 2025 at Phillips Hall 318 with 10 beginner level Japanese learners.

(4) Evaluation: I evaluated participants' achievement toward pre-determiend objectives.

Keywords: Instructional Design, the ADDIE Model, Backward Design, Sociocultural Approach
